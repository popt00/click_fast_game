Our Commitment

Quality products at everyday low prices – that's the promise and it's as true today more than 50 years later. We consider it both our mission and our privilege to give Canadians access to the products they need at the prices they can afford, both in stores and online





Our Business

B-Mart is honored to be one of Canada's largest employers. And we continue growing, by adding more services, including online shopping, home delivery, and pickup at stores and other locations. Every day, we help move our country's economy forward by working with more than 2,100 Canadian suppliers. We're also creating jobs and opportunities for people of all backgrounds and helping strengthen communities across the country.


B-Mart Canada

B-Mart has grown to more than 100,000 associates who serve our customers with pride and commitment, either online or in one of our more than 400 stores nationwide.
